### Basic Role Permissions:
- **Owner**: Full permissions (manage server, roles, channels, integrations, etc.)
- **Administrator**: Nearly full permissions, except deleting the server.
- **Moderator**: Manage messages, kick/ban members, and manage channels.
- **Bot Manager**: Manage bots, bot permissions, and bot commands.
- **VIP**: Access to exclusive channels and early access to announcements.

### General Member Permissions:
- **Read/Send Messages**
- **Join/Leave Voice Channels**
- **React to Messages**
- **Change Nickname**
